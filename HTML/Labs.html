Lab One - Using IBM Quantum Composer with Quantum Gates

Summary

Lab one is focused on using IBM's Quantum Composer to visualize the manipulation of quantum states. Students will get to run their code on real IBM quantum hardware, observing the effects of noise on the results of quantum circuits.
